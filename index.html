Q: Tell me about a time you had to deal with a difficult stakeholder.

At JPMorgan, a senior financial analyst kept bypassing the data team entirely — pulling raw tables directly from Redshift and building his own Excel-based reports. The problem was his numbers were diverging from the official dashboards because he was hitting unprocessed data before our transformation logic ran.

Rather than escalating it as a process violation, I sat down with him and walked through exactly where his numbers were coming from versus where ours were. Once he saw the specific transformation steps he was missing — currency normalization, transaction deduplication — he understood why the gap existed. I built him a dedicated view that matched his workflow but pulled from the curated layer.

Arcadian Infracom's cross-functional data work requires exactly that — meeting stakeholders where they are rather than enforcing process from above.
