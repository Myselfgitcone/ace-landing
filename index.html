At my previous role, a senior analyst kept bypassing the data team — pulling raw tables directly from Redshift and building his own Excel reports. His numbers were diverging from official dashboards because he was hitting unprocessed data before our transformation logic ran.

Rather than escalating it, I walked through exactly where his numbers differed. Once he saw the transformation steps he was missing — currency normalization, deduplication — he understood the gap. I built him a dedicated view matching his workflow but pulling from the curated layer.

Amazon's ownership principle is exactly that — meeting stakeholders where they are, not enforcing process from above.

How it works

Three steps.
Then forget it's there.

Set up once before your interview. Ace runs silently after that.

1

Paste resume + job description

Add your resume text, job description, and company in Settings. One-time per role. Ace uses this to personalize every answer.

2

Start Interview mode

Click Start. Ace collapses to a small bubble. Press ▶ Start timer to go live. Press Ctrl+H to hide instantly anytime.

3

Read the answer out loud

Ace hears the question and streams a structured answer in under 2 seconds. Read it naturally. Next question — repeat.
